# 🇳🇵 Nepal Law Commission Legal Archive

> **A comprehensive archive of publicly available legal documents from the Nepal Law Commission, organized into a single repository for legal research, education, AI development, natural language processing, and preservation of Nepal's legislative history.**

---

## Overview

The **Nepal Law Commission Legal Archive** is an effort to consolidate publicly available legal resources from the Nepal Law Commission into one organized repository.

The Nepal Law Commission hosts thousands of legal documents across numerous categories, making it difficult to download, search, and process them efficiently. This project brings those publicly available resources together into a structured collection that is easier to explore, study, and build upon.

Whether you're a law student, researcher, software developer, policymaker, journalist, or AI engineer, this repository provides a centralized source for accessing Nepal's legal texts.

The goal is simple:

> **Make Nepal's publicly available laws easier to access, search, study, preserve, and use for education and legal technology.**

---

# Vision

Legal information should be accessible to everyone.

This repository exists to help create an open foundation for legal education, computational legal research, and AI systems that can better understand Nepal's legal framework.

By bringing together publicly available legal documents in one place, this archive aims to reduce barriers to legal research while encouraging innovation in legal technology.

---

# Why This Repository Exists

The official Nepal Law Commission website contains a vast collection of legislation spanning decades.

Although comprehensive, downloading or processing every document individually can be challenging for researchers and developers working on:

- Legal search engines
- AI assistants
- RAG systems
- LLM datasets
- Academic research
- Data mining
- Machine learning
- NLP
- Digital preservation

This repository provides a centralized archive that simplifies those workflows.
